Career Development Centres

Addressing the employability gap is a national imperative, given the influx of youth entering the workforce annually and the simultaneous demand from businesses for skilled personnel. This initiative aligns with key national programs such as the National Skill Development Mission, National Urban Livelihood Mission, and Skill India. There is an urgent need for large-scale employability initiatives to meet this challenge effectively. .

Our Approach

Many existing livelihood programs fall short in their efforts to alleviate poverty, as they tend to focus solely on skills training. This narrow approach often overlooks essential elements such as unlocking beneficiaries' aspirations, understanding their vulnerabilities, and fostering a sense of social belonging. As a result, the skills acquired may not effectively lead to meaningful economic advancement. .

Career Empowerment

Career Development Centres are built around a more holistic and sustainable approach, significantly increasing the likelihood of achieving mutual benefits for both youth women and their communities. By integrating social and economic development and digital literacy we aim to create lasting impact and opportunities for all involved. .

Career Development Centre 1

GTT Foundation's CDC 1 is designed to train and place candidates effectively in various fields.

  • Equipped to support 600-1000 candidates per year.
  • Providing training in skills essential for career growth.
  • Partnerships with top industry recruiters for placement.

Each CDC is strategically located to ensure accessibility for candidates from all regions. The foundation is committed to empowering youth and bridging the gap between education and employment.

Career Development Centre 2

CDC 2 focuses on providing holistic training to candidates in a variety of sectors, preparing them for a successful career.

The training is designed to meet the needs of industry and equip candidates with the necessary skills to succeed.

  • Supports 600-1000 candidates per year for training and placement.
  • Specialization in technical and soft skills development.
  • Direct collaboration with employers for job placement.

Career Development Centre 3

CDC 3 is another key location where GTT Foundation provides career-oriented training and placement opportunities.
