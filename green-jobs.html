GTT Foundation

Empowering Youth for Green Jobs

GTT Foundation is preparing the youth for green jobs by equipping them with essential skills for the burgeoning Electric Vehicles (EV) industry. This program aims to provide the expertise needed to thrive in this sunrise sector.

Classroom and Lab Training
Awareness for EV Sector Roles
On-the-Job Training
Mentoring and Employment Support
Classroom and Lab Training

Structured training in classrooms and labs provides participants with the knowledge and skills to handle diverse roles in the Electric Vehicles industry.

Awareness for EV Sector Roles

Creating awareness and building expertise to manage roles within the Electric Vehicles sector, empowering youth to succeed in this industry.

On-the-Job Training

Hands-on experience through on-the-job training ensures participants are industry-ready and confident in their skills.

Mentoring and Employment Support

Participants receive up to 6 months of mentoring after placement, ensuring long-term success in the Electric Vehicles industry.

GREENWAYS

ELECTRIC VEHICLES DOMAIN

The GreenWays program equips youth with skills for the growing Electric Vehicles sector, offering training, hands-on experience, and placement with top industry leaders.
