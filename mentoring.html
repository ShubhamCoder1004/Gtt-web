Empowering Through Mentoring

At GTT Foundation, we believe in the power of mentorship to guide individuals towards personal and professional success. Our mentoring programs offer invaluable support and resources to help people achieve their career goals and aspirations.

Mentoring Support

At GTT Foundation, mentoring support is the core of our offerings – for both employment and livelihood-oriented programmes. Participants of the programme are supported by counsellors and mentors for adapting to work environment and handholding them for settling down in their chosen paths. Apart from sharing regular career tips and guidance, personalized support is also extended to the interested participants. Our experienced counsellors and mentors are available on phone, in-person, and through webinars to share their insights on career expectations by employers and answer queries on career-related subjects without any prejudice or judgement.

  • Mentoring support with career guidance, personalized advice, and help adapting to work environments.

Career Counselling

The journey with skilling programmers at GTT Foundation begins with our participants being assessed for their strengths and interests using the digital career management platform Skills Alpha in order to arrive at customized career pathways. Skills Alpha helps participants to explore different career options in different domains and identify the aspirational careers suitable for them and the skill gaps that are required to be addressed. Our counsellors interact with the participants and share the career prospects in different sectors enabling the participants to decide upon the appropriate learning pathways.

Curious About Us? Find Answers Here!

The GTT Foundation, formerly known as the Natarajan Education Society (NES), was established by Dr. Ganesh Natarajan and Dr. Uma Ganesh. Our mission is to support underserved segments of society in achieving their aspirations for sustainable livelihoods and enhanced employability through skill development and technology solutions.
There are various ways to get involved, including volunteering or partnering with us. For more information on how you can contribute to our mission, please visit our Our Story page or contact us directly.
We focus on projects designed to increase market-relevant skills and enhance economic productivity through skilled individuals and innovation. Our initiatives aim to provide effective skill development and technological solutions to transform the futures of many young Indians.
Our approach involves creating and implementing innovative ideas and using cutting-edge technology to deliver skill development programs. We are committed to applying global standards in pedagogy to ensure that our programs meet the highest quality benchmarks.
Our programs are designed for underserved individuals who seek to improve their skills and employability. We particularly focus on young Indians who aspire to build a better future through enhanced skills and sustainable livelihoods.
We measure success through various metrics, including the number of individuals who gain market-relevant skills, their improved economic productivity, and the overall impact on their livelihoods. We also track the effectiveness of our programs through feedback from participants and other stakeholders.
