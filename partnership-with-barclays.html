EMPOWERMENT

Empowering Communities Through Barclays LifeSkills Programme

GTT Foundation is a partner for Barclays LifeSkills Programme,supporting those facing challenges to be equipped with skills and provide them with avenues for employment. .

The focus is on individuals with limited access to formal skills and social capital. GTT Foundation has been partnering with Barclays since 2016 on the LifeSkills programme to support people from the community with relevant work-ready skills.

We have trained, placed, and mentored hundreds and thousands of people into work across the country, collaborating with more than 3000 colleges and academic institutions, while continuing to work with communities to provide them with employment opportunities.
