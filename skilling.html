    Employability programmes

    GTT Foundation’s Employability Programmes are well researched, demand driven and customized for different target segments namely women from the urban/rural locations, Tier2/3college students, persons with disabilities and LGBTQ+ communities. Programmes are domain focused as well as centered around soft skills required at workplace and are delivered in bi-lingual format, enriched with case studies, mock interviews and role plays. The Employability programmes are supported by the digital career management system - Skills Alpha- for assessment, self-learning, coaching and knowledge sharing and career navigation.

    • Well-researched, demand-driven, and customized employability programmes.
    • Focus on domain-specific skills and workplace soft skills.
    • Delivered in a bi-lingual format with case studies, mock interviews, and role plays.

    Green Jobs- Greenways

    The thrust of the countries around the world to work towards mitigation of climate change and green initiatives as per the UN mandate has impacted jobs too. With the Govt of India’s stated goal of cutting carbon emissions by 50% by 2023 and creating 3.5 crore jobs across multiple sectors by 2047, GTT Foundation is aligned with this goal to prepare the youth and women for green jobs. One of its major initiatives in this category is to equip members of the community with relevant skills to find suitable green jobs in the automobiles sector. The Electric Vehicles project- Green Ways launched by GTT Foundation is aimed at skilling and placing 50,000 students in green jobs by 2030.

    • Aligned with the global goal of mitigating climate change and creating green jobs.
    • Supports India's target of reducing carbon emissions by 50% by 2023 and creating 3.5 crore jobs by 2047.
    • Focused on preparing youth and women for green jobs, particularly in the electric vehicles sector.
    • The Electric Vehicles project - Green Ways aims to skill and place 50,000 students in green jobs by 2030.
