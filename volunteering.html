Empowering Through Volunteering

GTT Foundation has rich experience in curating a wide range of Volunteering programmes aimed at supporting the career needs of the participants of our programmes.

    GTT Foundation hosts impactful events designed to inspire and engage our community. From workshops to networking gatherings, each event fosters collaboration and connection, empowering individuals to grow and thrive together.Employees from various organizations and individuals who wish to contribute their time and skills for the benefit of participants of our programs could participate in the volunteering engagements. Our initiatives foster teamwork and communication, empowering volunteers to contribute their best in a vibrant environment.

    • Opportunities to volunteer in community development projects.
    • Engagement in global outreach programs and events.
    • Training and support for effective volunteering.
