Unlocking Potential through Skilling, Livelihood, and Mentoring

Unemployment and under employment are major challenges faced by the marginalized segments of the country. GTT Foundation was established in the year 2012 specifically to address these twin issues and empower the vulnerable communities to become economically independent through skilling programmes leading to employment and livelihood options.

ABOUT US

Bridging the Skill Gap

Bridging the skill gap by offering high-quality skill development programs to underserved communities across the country.

GTT Foundation was founded by Dr. Ganesh Natarajan and Dr. Uma Ganesh with a mission to empower underserved segments of society in their pursuit of sustainable livelihoods and enhanced employability.

We have brought together a team of passionate professionals committed to devising and implementing innovative strategies for skill development and technological solutions, thereby transforming the futures of countless young Indians.

OUR WORK

Explore Our Key Initiatives

Discover how GTT Foundation is making a difference through impactful partnerships and projects. From empowering communities to supporting women in tech, explore our work across various sectors and initiatives.

Empowering Communities: Our Partnership with Barclays

Since 2016, GTT Foundation has partnered with Barclays on the LifeSkills programme, providing community members with essential work-ready skills. This collaboration has enabled us to train, place, and mentor thousands across the country, working with over 3000 colleges and academic institutions to enhance employment opportunities.

Bridging the Employability Gap: The Khushi Center Initiative

The Khushi Project aims to address the critical issue of employability by empowering youth and women in rural communities. Aligned with national missions such as the National Skill Development Mission and Skill India, this initiative focuses on a holistic approach to poverty alleviation and economic development.

Explore Green Jobs: Building a Sustainable Future

Green jobs focus on reducing environmental impact and promoting conservation. Careers in renewable energy, energy efficiency, and environmental conservation play a vital role in creating a sustainable future.

Empowering Digital Citizens: Our Digital Support Initiative

Our Digital Support initiative empowers citizens from low-income communities to become vigilant digital citizens, alert digital customers, and enthusiastic digital learners. This program enhances digital literacy and helps underserved populations navigate the digital landscape.

Empowering Women in Tech

Our Women in Tech initiative is dedicated to increasing female representation and participation in technology fields. We provide training, mentorship, and opportunities to help women advance their careers in tech.

Empowering Futures

Providing impactful training programs to equip students from Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu with essential skills for BFSI, IT/ITES, and HC industries. Our mission is to ensure students are placement-ready and thrive in today's dynamic job market.

Transforming Lives

GTT Foundation, in partnership with Swades Foundation, supports skilling and employment for youth in Raigad and Nashik districts, Maharashtra. Focusing on holistic development, we engage with students from college entry to successful employment, empowering communities through

WeChimni: Empowering Connections

WeChimni is a digital platform connecting micro-entrepreneurs with corporate entities, supported by skilled volunteers. It streamlines customer discovery, facilitates seamless B2B and B2C transactions, and enhances revenue opportunities for women beneficiaries via web and mobile apps. Learn more at www.wechimni.org.

Networking

This Programme supports women from marginalized communities in Gujarat and Maharashtra through a Sustainable Lifestyle Program. Offering training in eco-friendly fashion production, along with backward and forward linkages, counseling, and ongoing support, it empowers women to sustain livelihoods, thrive as entrepreneurs, and drive inclusive economic development.

Bridging the Employability Gap

With millions of youth entering the workforce annually, addressing the employability gap is critical. This initiative aligns with national programs like the Skill India Mission, National Skill Development Mission, and National Urban Livelihood Mission, driving large-scale efforts to equip youth with skills and meet industry demands effectively.

Our Engagements

GTT Foundation (GTTF) has developed the right tools, technologies, processes and domain specific content to implement the employability programmes. Trainers are handpicked and certified before their deployment at various locations in the country where the employability programmes are implemented. Not only do we train and find suitable placement opportunities to the participants of our programme, we also provide them with counselling and mentoring support for a year after starting off on their jobs in order to help them adapt to their work environment.

Empowering Women for Sustainable Income Generation

Empowering women for sustainable income generation is a critical aspect of social and economic development. By providing women with the necessary skills, resources, and opportunities, we help them achieve financial independence, improve their quality of life, and contribute to the overall well-being of their communities.

Development of Sustainable Livelihoods and Entrepreneurial Endeavors

Our programs are designed to cultivate sustainable livelihoods and entrepreneurship among women, tailored to their financial needs and the burgeoning opportunities for remote work facilitated by digital resources.

Career Advancement for Socio-Economically Disadvantaged Segments

We strive to elevate the career prospects of individuals facing socio-economic challenges by equipping them with industry-ready skills, providing mentorship, and offering continuous support throughout their professional journeys.

Transformation of Rural Communities through Employability Solutions

Our skills interventions target underserved regions of India, bridging the gap between aspirations and tangible outcomes for rural youth, from early school dropouts to individuals in their upper middle ages.

Nurturing Innovation

We champion the spirit of innovation amongst the youth, with a specific emphasis on leveraging digital technology for social impact, thereby accelerating socio-economic transformation in underserved communities.
