Vision & Mission

Ankur Programme.

The Ankur Programme empowers women from marginalized communities through a Sustainable Lifestyle Program, providing training in eco-friendly fashion production. It helps them become self-sufficient entrepreneurs, contributing to sustainable and inclusive economic development in Gujarat and Maharashtra.

  • Empowering Women: Provide training to women from marginalized communities.
  • Eco-Friendly Production: Teach sustainable and eco-friendly fashion production techniques.
  • Entrepreneurship: Help women become self-sufficient entrepreneurs.

Barclays LifeSkills Programme

GTT Foundation is a partner for the Barclays LifeSkills Programme, supporting those facing challenges to be equipped with skills and provide them with avenues for employment. The focus is on individuals with limited access to formal skills and social capital. GTT Foundation has been partnering with Barclays since 2016 on the LifeSkills programme to support people from the community with relevant work-ready skills.

We have trained, placed, and mentored hundreds and thousands of people into work across the country, collaborating with more than 3000 colleges and academic institutions, while continuing to work with communities to provide them with employment opportunities.

  • Skills Development: Providing relevant work-ready skills to those in need.
  • Training and Placement: Training and placing individuals across the country.
  • Collaboration with Institutions: Partnering with 3000+ colleges to facilitate employment.

Partnership with Swades Foundation

GTT Foundation partners with Swades Foundation to support the skilling and employment needs of the youth in colleges in the districts of Raigad and Nashik in Maharashtra. Employability of the community is an integral part of its holistic 360-degree development approach. GTT Foundation engages with the youth from the time they enter college and continues the employability interventions until they are placed into work.

  • Holistic Development: Engaging youth in colleges and providing ongoing employability support.
  • Skilling and Employment: Focus on skill development and job placements.
  • Partnership Impact: Helping youth in Raigad and Nashik become employable and succeed in their careers.

Partnership with ATOS Syntel

The programme is aimed at uplifting students through impactful training programs that ensure they are placement-ready. Our mission is to empower students from Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu with the requisite skills to be part of the BFSI, IT/ITES, and HC industries, ensuring their success in today’s dynamic job market and paving the way for bright futures.

  • Placement-Ready Training: Providing students with the skills necessary for placement in key industries.
  • Industry Focus: Preparing students for careers in BFSI, IT/ITES, and HC industries.
  • Empowerment and Success: Empowering students to succeed in today’s competitive job market.
