Our Partnership with WeChimni

WeChimni serves as a transformative digital platform that bridges micro-entrepreneurs with corporate entities, while harnessing the expertise of volunteers from these organizations to provide continuous support to beneficiaries.

This innovative platform facilitates the discovery of customers and establishes seamless connections to address their specific needs, enabling all stakeholders to monitor transactions effortlessly and amplify revenue-generating opportunities for women beneficiaries.

WeChimni supports both B2B and B2C interactions, allowing for business transactions via both web and mobile applications. Discover more at www.wechimni.org .
