    Livelihood Programmes Ankur

    GTT Foundation’s Livelihood Programmes – Ankur - sow the seed for micro-entrepreneurship and self-employment amongst women. Women who are keen to supplement the household incomes but are constrained to take up 9-5 jobs are encouraged to acquire skills that enable them to become self-sufficient and economically independent. Our structured women’s empowerment programmes are aimed at equipping women with relevant skills in fashion design, handicrafts, and food. They are also supported to form Self Help Groups (SHGs) and develop entrepreneurial capabilities to sustain their livelihoods.

    Providing skill development opportunities in fashion design, handicrafts, and food.

    Structured programs aimed at making women economically independent and self-sufficient.

    Focus on holistic empowerment with relevant domain skills and entrepreneurial development.
